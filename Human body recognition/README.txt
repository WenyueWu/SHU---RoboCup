main.cpp
Based on the robocup project group at Shanghai University.
Processing the image information collected by Kinect based on C++.
Some of the output and comments are in Chinese.

Adjusts the length and width of the image by recognizing the human skeleton. The effect of showing hand-drawn designs of clothes can be achieved by by planting pictures in png format. All images used here are web images.
